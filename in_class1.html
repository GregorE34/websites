Longshot Features (static)

The page is static because while it is engaging it does not have any aspects that update without refreshing the page and doesn't seem to utilize any sort of database. I love the design and side-scrolling aspect of this website. I think the starkness of the black and white really works. I'm immediately intrigued about the work they are doing.


Oh My Rockness (dynamic)

This page is dynamic because I can add shows and bands to my list without refreshing the page. It utilizes a database to keep track of my choices. I like the functionality and the way it allows me to keep track of live music but I hate the editorial style. I also find the design to just OK but appreciate how functional it is on mobile.
